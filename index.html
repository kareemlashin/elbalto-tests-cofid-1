Corona virus test

Based on CDC guidelines, we’ve prepared an assessment to help you determine what precautions you should take in the event coronavirus (COVID-19) impacts your community.

The assessment should take less than two minutes and will provide you with personalized prevention recommendations based on your answers.

Do you suffer from any of the following

  • Severe difficulty breathing (difficulty breathing or speaking)
  • Severe chest pain
  • Having trouble getting up
  • Feeling disturbed and confused
  • Loss of consciousnes

Do you suffer from any of the following

  • Shortness of breath
  • Inability to lie down due to difficulty breathing
  • Chronic health condition that you cannot control due to your current respiratory

Do you suffer from any of the following

  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath
  • Sore throat or congestion

What is Corona virus?

Corona viruses are a large group of viruses that may cause disease in animals and humans. It is known that a number of coronaviruses cause human respiratory infections that range from common colds to more severe diseases such as the Middle East Respiratory Syndrome and severe acute respiratory syndrome (SARS). The newly discovered Coronavirus causes the Corvid-19 virus.
